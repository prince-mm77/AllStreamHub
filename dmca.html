DMCA & Copyright Policy

Our website operates strictly as an automated directory index and bookmarking catalog. We do not host, store, or stream any digital video, software, or copyrighted files on our local servers.

We respect the intellectual property rights of others and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If your copyrighted work has been compiled, parsed, or indexed on our platform and you wish for it to be removed from our library search matrix, you may submit a formal request.

1. Take-Down Notice Requirements

To clear and process your copyright claim efficiently, your notification must be structured as a formal written communication containing the following mandatory elements:

  1. Authorization: A physical or electronic signature of a person authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
  2. Identification of Work: Clear identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed, or a comprehensive list if multiple items are covered.
  3. Target Locator: Identification of the specific material or external link that is to be removed, including the exact URL(s) listed on our catalog page.
  4. Contact Information: Information reasonably sufficient to permit us to contact you, such as an address, telephone number, and an official company email address.
  5. Good Faith Statement: A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  6. Accuracy Statement: A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner.

2. Content Removal Timeline

Upon receipt of a valid, properly formatted DMCA notice containing all six pieces of verification listed above, our compliance team will investigate and remove the entry/bookmark links from our index system within 48 to 72 business hours.
